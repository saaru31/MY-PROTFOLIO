FPV Car

Objective of my project:


The FPV (First Person View) Car project integrates ESP-8266, ESP32-CAM, and BLDC motors to create a remote-controlled car with live video streaming and internet control
capabilities. Users can remotely operate the car from anywhere with internet access, controlling its movement via a mobile app while receiving live video feedback from the
onboard camera.This setup allows for real-time interaction and exploration of remote environments without physical presence, making it suitable for surveillance, exploration,
and educational purposes. The project's focus on connectivity and video streaming enhances user experience,enabling seamless remote control and monitoring over long distances
